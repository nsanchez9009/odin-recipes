Lemon Ricotta Pancakes

Description:

These pancakes are so light, they're going to float off the plate! and you don't even have to whip the egg whites.

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup cold water or milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon grated lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/8 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 cup self-rising flour
  • 2 tablespoons self-rising flour

Directions:

Steps:

  1. Whisk water and baking soda together in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add ricotta cheese, lemon zest, vegetables oil, sugar, egg, and vanilla. Whisk until smooth, breaking up lumps of cheese as you mix.
  3. Add melted butter, lemon juice, and 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons self-rising flour. Whisk together, stirring until most of flour disappears into the batter.
  4. Let batter sit at room temperature about 15 minutes.
  5. Heat lightly greased cast iron skillet or griddle over medium-high heat.
  6. Portion out 1/4 cup scoops of batter onto skillet (cook in batches). When bubbles begin to form on the surface and the edges of the pancakes start to look dry (2 to 3 minutes), flip and cook other side until cooked through, 2 to 3 minutes.
  7. Transfer to a warm plate.

Notes:

This recipe makes 2 large or 4 small servings. To make your own self-rising flour (2 cups worth): Sift together 2 cups all-purpose flour, with 2 teaspoons baking powder, and 1 teaspoon fine salt.
